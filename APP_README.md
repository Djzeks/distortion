## 📥 Installation Guide

### For Android Users

**Method 1: Using Chrome**
1. Open the app at: `https://djzeks.github.io/distortion/app.html`
2. Tap the menu icon (⋮) at the top right
3. Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Confirm the installation
5. The app will appear on your home screen

**Method 2: Using Samsung Internet or Firefox**
- Follow the same steps as Chrome
- Look for "Add to Home Screen" option

### For iOS Users

**Method 1: Using Safari**
1. Open the app at: `https://djzeks.github.io/distortion/app.html`
2. Tap the Share button (arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Enter a name (or keep default "Distortion")
5. Tap "Add" at the top right
6. The app will appear on your home screen

**Note:** iOS PWAs don't show the install prompt, but you can manually add them to home screen using the method above.

## 🎯 How to Use

### Booking a Service
1. Tap the **"Book"** tab at the bottom
2. Select the service type you need
3. Choose your preferred date
4. Fill in your details
5. Describe your event
6. Select your budget range
7. Tap **"Submit Booking Request"**
8. Your request will be sent via WhatsApp

### Viewing Portfolio
1. Tap the **"Works"** tab
2. Browse through recent projects
3. Tap any image or video to view in full screen
4. Swipe or use the close button to return

### Contacting Us
1. Tap the **"Profile"** tab
2. Tap **"WhatsApp"** to chat directly
3. Or tap **"Email"** to send an email

### Common Issues

**"Install" button not showing?**
- The prompt appears after you've visited the app once
- Try refreshing the page
- Ensure you're using a compatible browser

**App not loading offline?**
- Give it 30 seconds to cache content on first visit
- Try refreshing the page once
- Make sure Service Worker is enabled in browser settings

**Portfolio images not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Clear app cache and reload
- Try updating the app
